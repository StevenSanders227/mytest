Seafood Gumbo

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup bacon dripping
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped celery
  • 1 large onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1 large green bell pepper, coarsely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound andouille sausage, sliced
  • 3 quarts water
  • 6 cubes beef bouillon
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon Cajun seasoning blend
  • 4 bay leaves
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme leaves
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 4 teaspoons file powder, divided
  • 2 tablespoons bacon drippings
  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen cut okra, thawed
  • 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
  • 1 pound lump crabmeat
  • 3 pounds uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. First make the roux. Whisk together flour and 3/4 cup bacon drippings in a large saucepan over medium-low heat until smooth. About 20 to 30 minutes. Cook roux, whisking constantly until it turns a golden brown color. Remove from heat.
  2. Finely chop celery, onion, green bell pepper, and garlic.
  3. Stir roux, sausage, and vegetables together. Cook at medium-low heat, stir constantly, 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Combine water and beef bouillon in pot and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Let bouillon cubes dissolve, then whisk roux mixture into the boiling water.
  5. Reduce heat to a simmer and mix in sugar, salt, hot pepper sauce, Cajun seasoning, bay leaves, thyme, stewed tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour; add 2 teaspoons of file gumbo powder after 45-minutes.
  6. Reduce heat to a simmer and mix in sugar, salt, hot pepper sauce, Cajun seasoning, bay leaves, thyme, stewed tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Simmer soup over low heat for 1 hour; mix in 2 teaspoons of file gumbo powder after 45-minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, melt 2 tablespoons bacon drippings in a skillet over medium heat. Add okra and vinegar and cook for 15 minutes; remove okra with a slotted spoon, and stir into the simmering gumbo.
  8. Mix in crabmeat, shrimp, and Worcestershire sauce, and simmer until flavors have blended, 45 more minutes. Stir in 2 more teaspoons of file gumbo powder just before serving.

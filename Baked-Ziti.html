Baked Ziti

Ingredients

  • 16ox box of any Ziti pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • 8oz of Sour Cream
  • 1/2 Cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Egg (Beaten)
  • 2 Jars Of Ragu Parmesan Romano Sauce
  • 8oz bag of shredded Mozzarella
  • Yellow Onion
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic (Minced)

Seasonings

  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian Seasoning

    • Season to your heart is happy.

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350F
  2. Heat a large pot of salted water. Bring to a boil.
    (For every 2 quarts of water, add one tablespoon of salt)
    Add the pasta and cook it at a rolling boil, uncovered, until the pasta is edible, but still firm to the bite.
    (Pasta will still cook while it's in the oven.)
    Remove from the heat.
    Drain the pasta, then toss with a little olive oil.
    Put to the side.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, ground beef and seasonings in a skillet and brown it.
    Remove from heat, then drain the fat.
  4. In a bowl, combine the sour cream, Parmesan cheese and egg in a large bowl.
    Mix well.
  5. Add in the ground beef, cooked noodles and Sauce .
    Mix well.
  6. Add the mixture into a 9x13 ungreased glass pan
  7. Spread the second jar of sauce over the noodles.
    Just enough to cover with a thin layer.
  8. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake uncovered at 350 F for 20-25 minutes.

Notes

  • This recipe can be halved. Use half of the ingredients and bake in a 8x8 glass pan for 20 minutes.
  • My kids love cheese. So feel free to experiment with whatever cheese you prefer.
  • You can use different type of ground meat.
    (Turkey, Sausage, or chicken...)
  • My kids aren't fans of Ricotta cheese. I use sour cream instead.
  • The egg is optional it helps for a binding but is not needed.
  • After you boil the pasta, you can cook it right away or you can keep it in the refrigerator (covered with foil) to be baked later. If you do place it in the refrigerator, you should bake it for 45 minutes.
